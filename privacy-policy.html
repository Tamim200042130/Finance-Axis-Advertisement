Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2024. FinanceAxis ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Data We Collect

We collect financial data you manually input, including transactions, budget goals, and account balances. If you sign in, we collect your email address and profile name.

2. Third-Party Services

We use the following services to provide functionality:

  • Firebase: For cloud synchronization and authentication.
  • Google Drive API: To allow you to backup data to your own account. We only access files created by our App.
  • AdMob: To display ads to free users.
  • Google Play Billing: To process Pro subscriptions.

3. Data Ownership

Your financial data belongs to you. We do not sell or share your personal financial records with third parties.

4. Contact

If you have questions, contact us at support@tstationbd.com.
